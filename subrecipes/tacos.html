How to Make Tacos

Birria, a.k.a. tacos de birria, is a Mexican dish of meat (usually goat or beef) stewed in its braising liquid until it reaches a melt-in-your-mouth
texture. Traditionally, it gets its flavor from dried chile peppers.
Birria can be served alone as a stew or on corn tortillas.:

  • Cheese
